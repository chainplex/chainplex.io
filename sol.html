🚀 Why Solana?

The case for Solana as the leading high-performance smart contract platform

⚡
Unmatched Speed
Solana achieves 2,000–5,000+ real TPS in production, with a theoretical max of 65,000 TPS. Block times of ~400ms make it the fastest L1 for consumer applications.
💸
Near-Zero Fees
Average transaction fees of $0.0001–$0.001. Priority fees during congestion remain under $0.01 for most transactions — enabling micropayments and high-frequency trading at scale.
🌱
Ecosystem Growth
Over $6B+ in DeFi TVL, 100M+ wallets created, and category-leading apps in DEX (Jupiter), liquid staking (Jito/Marinade), perps (Drift), and DePIN (Helium).
🔧
Developer Momentum
Solana has the fastest-growing developer ecosystem after Ethereum. The Anchor framework, compressed NFTs, and state compression unlock new app categories impossible on other chains.
