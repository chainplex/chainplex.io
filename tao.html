What is Bittensor?

The Decentralized AI Marketplace

Bittensor is a decentralized protocol that creates a marketplace for machine intelligence. AI models (miners) compete to produce the best outputs for each subnet's specific task. Validators score their work, and TAO rewards flow to the best performers.

Think of it as Bitcoin's proof-of-work model, but instead of mining blocks, you're mining useful AI intelligence. Each subnet is a specialized AI economy with its own incentive structure.

TAO is the native token that powers the entire ecosystem — used for staking, validator delegation, and subnet registration. Like Bitcoin, TAO has a fixed max supply of 21 million with halvings every ~10.5M blocks.

🔑 Key Subnet Deep Dives

The most important subnets driving TAO value and ecosystem growth

💰
SN8 — Taoshi (Trading)
The most financially relevant subnet. Miners submit trading signals (long/short) across crypto and equities. Best-performing predictions earn the most TAO. Real money is at stake — miners have actual skin in the game.
Why it matters: This is DeFi + quant trading + AI all in one. If Taoshi signals work, it becomes a massive value-add and drives TAO demand.
Finance/Quant
🏋️
SN9 — Pretraining
Miners train foundation AI models from scratch and compete on benchmark performance. This is the "proof of useful work" at the core of Bittensor's value proposition — you earn TAO by making AI better.
Why it matters: If this produces models competitive with GPT/Gemini, it's a paradigm shift. The race to train open, decentralized AI starts here.
Model Training
⚙️
SN27 — Compute
Decentralized GPU marketplace. Miners rent H100s, A100s and other hardware to the network. Validators verify compute tasks are completed correctly. Competes directly with AWS, Google Cloud, CoreWeave.
Why it matters: GPU shortage is real. Unlocking idle compute globally at competitive prices could be worth billions.
GPU/Compute
🗄️
SN21 — FileTAO
Decentralized file storage on Bittensor. Miners store encrypted data reliably across the network. Think Filecoin but with TAO incentives and the Bittensor consensus mechanism ensuring quality.
Why it matters: AI needs data storage. Pairing SN9 (training) + SN21 (storage) creates a closed-loop decentralized AI infrastructure.
Storage
🌐
SN18 — Cortex.t API
The "AWS API Gateway" of Bittensor. Developers query Cortex.t and get back responses from the best miners across all subnets. Single API key, access to the whole Bittensor ecosystem.
Why it matters: This is the monetization layer. If developers pay TAO to use Cortex.t, it creates real economic demand for the token.
Developer API
📊
SN13 — Dataverse
Miners scrape, clean, and curate datasets from the web. This feeds directly into pretraining (SN9) and other subnets. Data is the oil of AI — Dataverse is the refinery.
Why it matters: High-quality data is the biggest bottleneck in AI. Decentralized data collection at scale could be transformative.
Data Pipeline
How to Participate in Bittensor

Three ways to earn TAO

🔬
Mine (Run AI Models)
Run AI models on a subnet and compete for emissions based on output quality. Requires: GPU (RTX 4090 min), Python/ML skills, subnet registration fee (burned TAO).

Risk: High. Bottom 50% earners get deregistered.
⚠️ Technical + Capital intensive
✅
Validate
Run a validator node that scores miner outputs and earns a percentage of subnet emissions. Requires: 1,000+ TAO stake, server infrastructure, technical knowledge.

Reward: 18-41% of emissions depending on subnet.
✅ Significant capital required
🏦
Delegate / Stake
Delegate your TAO to a trusted validator on taostats.io and earn a share of their rewards passively. No technical setup required, no minimum (though more = more rewards).

Yield: ~15-20% APY estimated.
🟣 Easiest option for investors
